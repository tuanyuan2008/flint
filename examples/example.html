How It Works

The Section Detector uses sophisticated algorithms to analyze web page layouts and identify distinct sections. By focusing on visual separation, content analysis, and layout patterns, we can break down complex websites into manageable components.

Our approach considers multiple factors including whitespace thresholds, content significance, and styling patterns to accurately identify section boundaries. This makes it possible to process large websites efficiently for AI applications.
